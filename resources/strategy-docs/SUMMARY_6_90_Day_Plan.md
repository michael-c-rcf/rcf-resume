# Summary: 30/60/90 Day Plan
**Document: 30_60_90_Day_Plan.md (17 pages)**

---

## Purpose
Specific, measurable deliverables for the first three months if hired. Shows you're ready to hit the ground running with clear objectives, action items, and success metrics for each phase.

---

## Guiding Principles

1. **Quick Wins:** Show value early (videos published, rep training delivered)
2. **Foundation Building:** Set up systems that scale (content calendar, certification framework)
3. **Feedback Loops:** Measure, learn, iterate (don't guess what works)
4. **Relationship Building:** Earn trust with reps, consultants, internal team

---

## Month 1: IMMERSION & FOUNDATION (Days 1-30)

### Week 1: Onboarding & Discovery
**Objectives:** Understand RCF product line, company culture, identify content opportunities

**Key Activities:**
- Meet RCF USA team, tour facility, receive product samples
- Hands-on demo of HDL, TT+, Compact, Subs
- RDShape training (get expert-level if not already)
- Review product documentation, listen to systems

**Deliverables:**
- Product knowledge self-assessment (written document)
- List of unique selling points for each product series
- 3 initial video ideas based on product insights

---

### Week 2: Stakeholder Interviews & Market Research
**Objectives:** Understand rep network, consultant relationships, market challenges

**Key Activities:**
- Internal interviews: Sales, marketing, technical support, president
- External interviews: 3-5 top-performing rep firms, 3-5 consultants who spec RCF, 2-3 consultants who DON'T spec RCF

**Deliverables:**
- Stakeholder interview summary (5-10 pages, themes + quotes)
- Prioritized list of content topics (based on real questions/objections)
- Rep enablement needs assessment (training gaps, sales tools)

---

### Week 3: Content Planning & YouTube Channel Launch
**Objectives:** Define content strategy, launch channel, publish first video

**Key Activities:**
- Content strategy document (audiences, pillars, 6-month calendar, metrics)
- YouTube channel setup (branding, playlists, SEO)
- Film, edit, publish first video (choose high-impact topic)

**Deliverables:**
- Content strategy document (shared with RCF leadership)
- YouTube channel live and branded
- First video published (target: 1,000 views in first week)

---

### Week 4: Rep Training & Academy Planning
**Objectives:** Deliver immediate value to rep network, plan certification program

**Key Activities:**
- Create rep training presentation v1.0 (slide deck, 60-90 min session)
- Deliver training to 2 pilot rep firms (gather feedback)
- Define RCF Academy 2.0 structure (tiers, curriculum, timeline, budget)

**Deliverables:**
- Rep training deck v1.0 (shareable PowerPoint/PDF)
- Training delivered to 2 rep firms (with feedback documented)
- RCF Academy 2.0 project plan (timeline, budget, curriculum outline)
- Month 1 summary report (wins, learnings, next steps)

---

## Month 2: QUICK WINS & PILOT PROGRAMS (Days 31-60)

### Week 5-6: Content Momentum & Community Building
**Objectives:** Publish 2-3 more videos, build YouTube audience, launch community

**Key Activities:**
- Publish Video 2: "Sound System Design 101 Ep.1: How Many Speakers?"
- Publish Video 3: "RCF TT+ Series: Active vs. Passive Explained"
- (Stretch goal) Video 4: "Project Spotlight: Bethesda Christian Church"
- Create RCF Designers Facebook Group or Discord server

**Deliverables:**
- 2-3 videos published (total: 3-4 videos live by end of Month 2)
- 500+ YouTube subscribers
- Community platform launched (50+ members)

---

### Week 7: Academy Beta Launch
**Objectives:** Build first Academy module, launch beta with 50 users

**Key Activities:**
- Record Bronze tier Modules 1-3 (Product overview, installation, system setup)
- Set up LMS platform (Teachable/Thinkific trial)
- Upload videos, create quizzes (10 questions each, auto-graded)
- Email reps + community: "Be among first 50 to earn RCF Installer certification"

**Deliverables:**
- 3 Academy modules live (Bronze tier, beta version)
- 50 beta users enrolled
- Feedback survey (sent to users after Module 1)

---

### Week 8: AI SmartSpec Planning & Prototyping
**Objectives:** Define MVP features, create prototype, pitch for budget approval

**Key Activities:**
- Document user stories and MVP feature set
- Create wireframes/mockups (Figma, Sketch, or hand-drawn)
- Test GPT-4 Vision with sample floor plans (proof of concept)
- Get quotes from 2-3 dev shops, create budget proposal

**Deliverables:**
- SmartSpec product requirements document (PRD)
- Wireframes/mockups (visual representation)
- Budget proposal + pitch deck (present to RCF leadership)

---

## Month 3: SCALE & ITERATE (Days 61-90)

### Week 9-10: Content Optimization & Rep Expansion
**Objectives:** Analyze video performance, expand rep training, hit 1,000 subscribers

**Key Activities:**
- Analyze YouTube analytics (identify top/underperforming videos)
- Adjust content calendar based on data
- Publish 2 more videos (data-driven topics)
- Deliver training to 3-5 additional rep firms
- Create "Rep Toolkit" resource hub

**Deliverables:**
- 2 videos published (total: 5-6 by end of Month 3)
- 1,000+ YouTube subscribers
- Training delivered to 5+ rep firms total
- Rep Toolkit resource hub (Google Drive or RCF portal)

---

### Week 11: Academy Public Launch (Bronze Tier)
**Objectives:** Finalize Bronze certification, launch publicly, issue first certifications

**Key Activities:**
- Review beta feedback, improve content
- Add Modules 4-5 (Troubleshooting, RDNet basics) to Bronze tier
- Announce via YouTube, email, social media (target: 100 enrollments first week)

**Deliverables:**
- Bronze tier complete (5 modules, final exam, certification)
- 100+ enrollments (post-launch)
- 20+ Bronze certifications issued (first graduates)

---

### Week 12: SmartSpec Development Kickoff & Quarter Review
**Objectives:** Begin SmartSpec development (if approved), review 90-day performance

**Key Activities:**
- Sign contract with dev partner (if budget approved)
- Kick-off meeting (milestones, deliverables, timeline)
- Set up project management (Trello, Asana)
- Compile 90-day metrics: YouTube, Academy, Sales leads
- Create summary report, present to RCF leadership

**Deliverables:**
- SmartSpec development in progress (if approved)
- 90-day summary report (data-driven assessment)
- Quarter 2 plan (refined based on Q1 learnings)

## Flexibility & Adjustments

**Scenario A: "Focus on Rep Enablement" (Sales-Driven)**
- Increase rep training cadence (10+ firms in 90 days)
- Create more sales tools (templates, ROI calculators, battlecards)
- Reduce Academy/SmartSpec focus temporarily

**Scenario B: "Focus on Content Momentum" (Brand-Driven)**
- Publish 3 videos/month instead of 2
- Launch podcast or webinar series
- Delay Academy/SmartSpec to focus on audience growth

**Scenario C: "Focus on Certification" (Quality-Driven)**
- Fast-track Academy development (Silver tier by Day 90)
- Partner with industry associations (AES, NSCA)
- Reduce YouTube to 1/month, prioritize Academy content
