## Opening Statement (30 seconds)

**Memorize This:**
"I'm at a unique intersection: I've built real-world installed systems, I teach audio concepts through content creation, and I've developed workflows that scale knowledge. RCF has exceptional products but faces an awareness challenge among consultants and installers. I can solve that through technical content, training programs, and strategic use of AI tools—while continuing to design systems that showcase RCF's capabilities."

## Questions to Ask (Strategic Thinking)

### Must-Ask Questions:
1. **Product Roadmap:** "What's RCF's 3-year vision for installed systems? New products I should plan content around?"
2. **Rep Network:** "Where are gaps in rep enablement I can help fill?"
3. **Competitive Positioning:** "When RCF competes with d&b/Meyer, what's the common objection?"
4. **Content Investment:** "Is RCF ready to invest in content creation (budget, time)? Or prove-it-first?"
5. **Success Metrics:** "What would make this role a home run in Year 1?"

### Follow-Up Questions (Show Depth):
- "What does a 'great rep' look like at RCF USA? Examples I can learn from?"
- "Are there markets where RCF consistently wins? Where we struggle?"
- "How do RCF USA and RCF Italy collaborate? Can we share content globally?"

## Objection Handling (Quick Responses)

### "Content takes time away from design work."
**Response:** "Design work IS content. Every project becomes a case study. Batch recording + templates = sustainable 2 videos/month while maintaining full design productivity. Framework: 70% design, 30% content—not doubling workload, leveraging existing work."

### "What if content doesn't drive sales?"
**Response:** "We measure from day one. 90-day pilot with clear KPIs: 500+ subscribers, 20+ inquiries, 3+ sales attributed. Low upfront cost (<$5K equipment), high learning value. Even 1 additional $100K project = 20x ROI."

### "Can RCF compete technically with d&b/Meyer?"
**Response:** "Absolutely. Gap is perception, not performance. Content closes that gap through objective comparisons, consultant testimonials, technical deep-dives. RCF HDL matches d&b V-Series in SPL/coverage at 40% lower cost. Question isn't 'Can RCF compete?'—it's 'Why overpay when RCF meets the spec?'"

### "YouTube is saturated. How will we break through?"
**Response:** "General audio content is crowded. Niche content for professional installers is underserved. By targeting specific searches ('church sound system design'), we capture consultants actively looking for solutions. Plus, RCF has built-in audience: rep network, existing customers. We're not starting from zero."

### "AI tools are unproven. Isn't this risky?"
**Response:** "It would be risky to ignore AI. Every brand is exploring this—question is who moves first. Our approach mitigates risk: MVP using existing AI (GPT-4), internal testing, iterate based on feedback. Low cost, high learning. Even if tool doesn't become a product, thought leadership positions RCF as innovator."

## Salary Discussion

### Research: $70K-$100K (hybrid design + content role)
### Position: Request $85K-$95K base + performance bonuses
### Flexibility: Open to lower base with clear performance incentives if budget constrained
### Growth Path: "What does advancement look like—senior designer, lead content strategist, director of education?"

**Don't Undersell:** You're building a competitive advantage, not just doing design work. Value compounds over time.

## Body Language & Presence

**Do:** Eye contact, active listening, lean in, smile naturally, firm handshake, take notes  
**Don't:** Fidgeting, crossed arms, phone checking, interrupting, filler words ("um," "like")

**Virtual Tips:** Camera at eye level, good lighting, clean background, test audio, look at camera not screen

"Thank you for this opportunity. I'm genuinely excited about RCF's potential. You have world-class products, but the market doesn't fully know that yet. I can help change that narrative—not through hype, but through education, transparency, and demonstrating RCF's capabilities in real-world projects.

I'm not just looking for a job; I'm looking to build something meaningful. I want to be part of the team that makes RCF the go-to brand for consultants, the first name that comes to mind when someone needs installed sound.

I have a clear vision: content that educates, tools that empower, and training that builds confidence. And I've already started building the systems—Airtable workflows, BOM automation, project management—that prove I can execute on that vision.

I'm ready to start tomorrow. Or rather, I'm ready to start preparing tonight so I can hit the ground running whenever you're ready.

What questions do you have for me?"
