# RCF Content Strategy: Detailed Implementation Plan
**For RCF USA Interview - February 17, 2026**

---

## Overview

This document outlines a comprehensive content strategy to position RCF as the most educational and accessible brand in professional installed sound. The goal: make consultants, installers, and integrators confident specifying RCF through free, high-quality technical education.

---

## I. YouTube Channel Strategy: "RCF Install TV"

### Channel Mission Statement
"Empowering audio professionals with practical knowledge to design, install, and tune RCF sound systems confidently."

### Target Audiences

**Primary (70% of content):**
- **Audio Consultants:** Need confidence specifying RCF in bid specs
- **AV Integrators:** Want technical know-how for successful installations
- **System Designers:** Seeking design workflows, best practices, product selection

**Secondary (20% of content):**
- **House of Worship Tech Directors:** Churches self-managing AV upgrades
- **School Facility Managers:** Education buyers researching solutions
- **Corporate AV Managers:** Conference centers, training facilities

**Tertiary (10% of content):**
- **Audio Students:** Learning system design fundamentals
- **DIY Enthusiasts:** Passionate hobbyists, potential brand advocates

### Production Workflow

**Pre-Production (Week 1):**
- Script outline (bullet points, not full transcript)
- Gather assets (photos, CAD files, RDShape designs)
- Schedule filming (batch record 2-3 videos in one session)

**Production (Week 2):**
- Film A-roll (Michael on-camera segments)
- Capture B-roll (product shots, installation footage, RDShape screen recordings)
- Record voiceover (if needed for screencasts)

**Post-Production (Week 3):**
- Edit video (cut for pacing, add graphics, music)
- Color grade + audio mix (professional polish)
- Create thumbnail (high-contrast, compelling)
- Write title + description (SEO-optimized, keywords)

**Publishing (Week 4):**
- Upload to YouTube (schedule for release date)
- Publish to website (embed video, add written summary)
- Cross-promote (LinkedIn, Facebook, rep newsletter)
- Monitor comments (engage with viewers, build community)

## II. RCF Academy 2.0: Certification Program

### Platform Architecture

**Technology Stack:**
- **LMS (Learning Management System):** Teachable, Thinkific, or custom WordPress + LearnDash
- **Video Hosting:** Vimeo (private, professional) or YouTube (unlisted, free)
- **Community Forum:** Discourse or Circle (dedicated discussion platform)
- **Certification Management:** Accredible or Credly (digital badges)

**User Experience:**
1. Sign up (free account, email required)
2. Browse learning paths (Installer, Designer, Expert)
3. Watch video modules (track progress)
4. Take quizzes (immediate feedback, must pass to advance)
5. Complete final exam (practical design submission)
6. Receive certificate (digital badge, PDF, shareable on LinkedIn)

---

### Certification Tiers

#### **Bronze: RCF Installer**
**Target Audience:** AV integrators, install technicians, church volunteers  
**Prerequisites:** None (entry-level)  
**Duration:** 8-10 hours (self-paced)  
**Cost:** Free (brand investment)

**Curriculum:**
- Module 1: RCF Product Overview (HDL, TT+, Compact, Subs, Amps)
- Module 2: Installation Best Practices (rigging, cabling, safety)
- Module 3: Basic System Setup (gain structure, speaker placement)
- Module 4: Troubleshooting Common Issues (no sound, feedback, networking)
- Module 5: RDNet Basics (monitoring, diagnostics)

**Assessment:**
- Quiz after each module (80% pass rate required)
- Final exam: 25 questions, multiple-choice, practical scenarios
- Certificate issued upon completion

**Value Proposition:**
- "Prove you know how to install RCF systems correctly."
- Badge for LinkedIn, resume credibility
- Access to installer-only resources (templates, support)

---

#### **Silver: RCF Designer**
**Target Audience:** Audio consultants, system designers, senior integrators  
**Prerequisites:** Bronze certification OR portfolio review  
**Duration:** 20-30 hours (self-paced)  
**Cost:** $99 (or free if RCF dealer/rep)

**Curriculum:**
- Module 1: Acoustics Fundamentals (room modes, RT60, coverage concepts)
- Module 2: RDShape Mastery (modeling, optimization, documentation)
- Module 3: Product Selection Strategy (choosing the right RCF series for application)
- Module 4: System Integration (DSP, amplifiers, networking, control)
- Module 5: Advanced Topics (cardioid subs, delay systems, acoustic treatment)
- Module 6: Project Documentation (specs, drawings, commissioning reports)

**Assessment:**
- Quizzes after each module (85% pass rate required)
- Final project: Submit a complete RDShape design for review
  - Scenario provided (e.g., "Design a 1,000-seat church sanctuary with $75K budget")
  - Evaluated on coverage, SPL, budget, documentation quality
- Video call interview (15 minutes, defend design choices)

**Certificate Requirements:**
- Pass all quizzes
- Final project approved by RCF instructor (Michael or senior consultant)
- Interview completed

**Value Proposition:**
- "Earn recognition as an RCF-certified system designer."
- Portfolio piece (showcase approved design)
- Priority support from RCF technical team

---

#### **Gold: RCF Expert**
**Target Audience:** Senior consultants, acoustic engineers, RCF trainers  
**Prerequisites:** Silver certification + 3+ completed RCF projects  
**Duration:** 40-60 hours (self-paced + mentorship)  
**Cost:** $299 (includes 1-on-1 mentorship sessions)

**Curriculum:**
- Module 1: Advanced Acoustics (psychoacoustics, EASE modeling, prediction accuracy)
- Module 2: Complex Rigging & Mechanical Design (flown arrays, structural analysis)
- Module 3: DSP Deep-Dive (FiRPHASE tuning, crossover design, measurement techniques)

- Module 5: Business of Audio (quoting, project management, client communication)

**Assessment:**
- Quizzes after each module (90% pass rate required)
- Portfolio submission: 3 completed RCF projects with documentation
- Final project: Design a complex multi-zone system (arena or performing arts center)
- Capstone presentation (30-minute recorded video, present design to panel)
- Peer review (other Gold candidates critique your work)

**Certificate Requirements:**
- Pass all quizzes
- Portfolio approved
- Capstone presentation scored 85%+
- Peer review participation

**Value Proposition:**
- "Join the elite tier of RCF-certified experts."

## III. AI Design Assistant: "RCF SmartSpec"

### Product Vision
"An AI-powered web tool that helps anyone—consultants, integrators, churches—design competent RCF sound systems in minutes."

---

### Core Features

#### **Phase 1: MVP (6 months)**

**Feature 1: Floor Plan Analysis**
- Upload JPG/PDF of venue floor plan
- AI (GPT-4 Vision) identifies dimensions, seating areas, stage location
- Suggests speaker placement zones

**Feature 2: Guided Product Selection**
- Input room dimensions (length × width × height)
- Select application (church, theater, school, corporate, sports)
- Input budget range ($10K, $25K, $50K, $100K+)
- AI recommends 3 RCF systems (good/better/best)

**Feature 3: Quick Quote Generation**
- Display line-item list (speakers, subs, amplifiers, accessories)
- Estimate total cost (dealer pricing)
- Generate PDF quote (brandable, shareable)

**Feature 4: Export to RDShape**
- Download RDShape file with suggested system pre-loaded
- User refines in RDShape (coverage optimization, tuning)

**Technology Stack:**
- Frontend: React.js (web app)
- Backend: Python (Flask or FastAPI)
- AI: OpenAI GPT-4 Vision API + custom prompts
- Database: PostgreSQL (user accounts, designs, product catalog)
- Hosting: AWS or Vercel (scalable)

**Budget (MVP):**
- Development: $20K-$30K (outsource to dev shop or hire contractor)
- API costs: $500/month (OpenAI usage)
- Hosting: $100/month (AWS/Vercel)
- **Total Year 1:** ~$35K

---

#### **Phase 2: Integration (12 months)**

**Feature 5: Dealer Inventory Sync**
- Real-time stock availability (avoid recommending out-of-stock products)
- Lead times displayed (ship immediately vs. 4-6 weeks)

**Feature 6: RDShape Deep Integration**
- Direct export (one-click open in RDShape)
- Sync design changes back to SmartSpec (bidirectional)

**Feature 7: Project Collaboration**
- Share designs with clients, reps, consultants (view-only or edit access)
- Comment threads (feedback, revisions)
- Version history (track design iterations)

**Feature 8: Enhanced AI Tuning**
- Train AI on successful RCF installations (your Airtable project data)
- Improve recommendations based on real-world outcomes
- Personalized suggestions (if user has designed similar venues before)

---

#### **Phase 3: Advanced Features (18 months)**

**Feature 9: AR Mobile App**
- Point phone camera at room → see speaker placement overlay
- Adjust positions in real-time (drag speakers, view coverage)
- Export to RDShape for finalization

**Feature 10: Acoustic Prediction**
- AI analyzes room materials (hard surfaces, carpet, drapes)
- Predicts RT60, recommends acoustic treatment
- Integrates with RCF system design (account for room challenges)

**Feature 11: Multi-Language Support**
- Expand SmartSpec globally (Spanish, Italian, French, German, Portuguese)
- AI-powered translation (maintain technical accuracy)

**Feature 12: Voice Interface**
- "Design a church system for 500 seats with a $50K budget"
- AI asks clarifying questions, generates design via conversation

## V. Risk Mitigation & Contingencies

### Risk 1: Content Doesn't Drive Engagement

**Indicators:**
- Low subscriber growth (<100/month after 6 months)
- Poor view counts (<500 per video)
- No inbound leads mentioning content

**Mitigation:**
- Audience research (survey current viewers, understand needs)
- SEO analysis (optimize titles, descriptions, tags)
- Content pivot (focus on high-performing topics, drop underperformers)
- Paid promotion (YouTube ads targeting audio professionals)

**Contingency:**
- Shift resources to other initiatives (Academy, SmartSpec)
- Reduce publishing cadence (1 video/month vs. 2)
- Continue as brand-building long-term play (not immediate ROI)

---

### Risk 2: Certification Program Low Enrollment

**Indicators:**
- <100 Bronze certifications in first 6 months
- High dropout rate (>50% don't complete)
- Negative feedback on content quality

**Mitigation:**
- Marketing push (email campaigns to reps, consultants, integrators)
- Incentives (free for RCF dealers, discounts for bulk enrollments)
- Content improvement (shorter modules, better production quality)
- Partner with trade associations (AES, NSCA) for credibility boost

**Contingency:**
- Simplify tiers (combine Bronze/Silver, focus on quality over quantity)
- Offer in-person workshops (supplement online content)
- Use Academy as lead-gen tool (free access, upsell products/services)

---

### Risk 3: AI SmartSpec Technical Challenges

**Indicators:**
- Poor AI accuracy (recommendations don't make sense)
- Slow performance (users frustrated with tool)
- Security/privacy concerns (uploading floor plans)

**Mitigation:**
- Iterative development (start simple, add complexity gradually)
- Beta testing (50 users provide feedback before public launch)
- Human oversight (Michael reviews AI suggestions, trains model)
- Transparent messaging ("AI assistant, not replacement for expertise")

**Contingency:**
- Delay launch (ensure quality before release)
- Pivot to simpler tool (product selector vs. full design assistant)
- Focus on educational use case (training tool vs. production tool)
