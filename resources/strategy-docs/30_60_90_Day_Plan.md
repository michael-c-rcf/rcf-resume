# 30/60/90 Day Action Plan
**RCF USA Installed Systems Designer + Content Strategist**  
**Michael Curtis | If Hired: February 17, 2026**

---

## Overview

This plan outlines specific, measurable deliverables for the first three months. The goal is to demonstrate immediate value while building foundations for long-term impact. Each phase has clear objectives, action items, and success metrics.

**Guiding Principles:**
1. **Quick Wins:** Show value early (videos published, rep training delivered)
2. **Foundation Building:** Set up systems that scale (content calendar, certification framework)
3. **Feedback Loops:** Measure, learn, iterate (don't guess what works)
4. **Relationship Building:** Earn trust with reps, consultants, internal team

## Potential Adjustments Based on Priorities

**If RCF priorities shift:**

**Scenario A: "Focus on Rep Enablement" (Sales-Driven)**
- Increase rep training cadence (10+ firms in 90 days)
- Create more sales tools (proposal templates, ROI calculators, competitive battlecards)
- Reduce Academy/SmartSpec focus temporarily

**Scenario B: "Focus on Content Momentum" (Brand-Driven)**
- Publish 3 videos/month instead of 2
- Launch podcast or webinar series (additional content formats)
- Delay Academy/SmartSpec to focus on audience growth

**Scenario C: "Focus on Certification" (Quality-Driven)**
- Fast-track Academy development (Silver tier by Day 90)
- Partner with industry associations (AES, NSCA) for credibility
- Reduce YouTube publishing to 1/month, prioritize Academy content

**Flexibility is key.** This plan is a framework, not a rigid rulebook. Adjust based on:
- RCF leadership priorities
- Early wins (double down on what's working)
- Market feedback (if consultants want X, deliver X)
- Resource constraints (budget, time, support)
